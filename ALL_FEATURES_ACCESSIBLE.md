## 🧪 **How to Access Each Feature**

### **Step 1: Login to Patient Dashboard**
1. Go to `/login/patient`
2. Login with patient credentials
3. You'll see the dashboard with 6 tabs

### **Step 2: Navigate to Features**

#### **Pharmacy (🏪)**:
1. Click the **Pharmacy** tab
2. Search for medicines (e.g., "Paracetamol")
3. Filter by location/city
4. See availability and prices
5. Contact pharmacies directly

#### **Video Consultation (📹)**:
1. Click the **Video** tab
2. See how video consultations work
3. Book an appointment first
4. Doctor initiates video call at appointment time

#### **Other Features**:
- **Chatbot (🤖)**: AI medical assistant
- **Symptoms (🩺)**: Symptom checker
- **Metrics (📈)**: Health tracking
- **Overview (📊)**: Appointment summary
